Our Philosophy

Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools like ChatGPT are powerful learning assistants that, when used responsibly, can enhance your education. This policy outlines how we'll use AI to deepen learning while maintaining academic integrity.

Core Principle: AI should amplify your thinking, not replace it.

You must cite AI assistance when:

How to Cite:

In-text: (ChatGPT, personal communication, [date])

Process Documentation:

Include an "AI Use Statement" with assignments describing:

  • What prompts you used
  • How AI helped your process
  • What you changed or rejected
  • What is entirely your own work

Prohibited Uses

âœ— Not Acceptable

  • Submitting AI-generated text as your own work - This is plagiarism
  • Using AI to complete assessments - Unless explicitly allowed
  • Having AI write entire assignments - You must do the thinking
  • Using AI to avoid learning - Shortcuts prevent growth
  • Misrepresenting your abilities - Be honest about your skills
  • Sharing others' work with AI - Respect intellectual property

Consequences & Conversations

If AI Use Concerns Arise:

  1. First Instance: Educational conversation about proper use
  2. Second Instance: Redo assignment with closer guidance
  3. Continued Misuse: Parent conference and academic consequences

Remember: The goal is learning, not punishment. We'll work together to find the right balance.
